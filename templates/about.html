About Burgertunes

What is Burgertunes?

Burgertunes is a privacy-respecting frontend for YouTube Music.

Why even use Burgertunes?

- Privacy. We don't know who is using Burgertunes.

- No advertisements at all.

- Works without JavaScript. (JavaScript is required for playing albums and playlists)

Is Burgertunes open source?

Yes! Burgertunes is under GNU AGPL v3.
